Who Are We?

Equipping individuals with AI readiness skills empowers them to engage more confidently in AI-related conversations, recognize and challenge abuses of AI in both personal and civic contexts, and explore how AI can be meaningfully integrated into their personal and professional lives.

We are students at the University of Chicago in Chicago, developing curriculum material for the everyday community member to build basic-intermediate AI literacy, foregrounding lived experiences and community input to co-create interesting, relevant, and impactful curriculum.
