About

DemystifyAI Chicago is a student-led, grassroots initiative that aims to make artificial intelligence (AI) more accessible to the everyday community member. Our mission is to equip Chicago community members with practical AI and digital literacy skills, empowering individuals to confidently participate in our AI-integrated society while protecting themselves from AI risks.

We strive to:

  • Provide nonprofits with ready-to-use AI curriculum
  • Collaborate with nonprofits to co-create and integrate AI literacy resources into digital inclusion curriculum
  • Empower individuals to engage responsibly with an AI-driven society

Our modules include:

  • AI Foundations
  • Protecting Yourself When Engaging with AI
  • AI as a Tool - Practical Applications for Community Benefit
